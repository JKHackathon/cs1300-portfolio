Competitive Analysis

The alert banners in both Reddit and Spotify were very useful as they very clearly displayed that an action had been complete. While this feature was lacking in EdStem, the presence of a bright yellow star at least provided enough indication of the post being saved. I personally found the ability to immediate undo saves very useful, however, this likely will not apply to my application as I intend to have each item have a button that saves and unsaves. Filtering within saved posts in EdStem is a bit lacking, as only certain filters may be used, which has made finding specific posts more difficult in my experience.
